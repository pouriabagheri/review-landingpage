Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using Toilet Review.

How accurate is the location data?

Our app uses advanced GPS technology combined with user-submitted data to provide highly accurate restroom locations. We continuously update our database with user feedback to ensure the most precise information is available.

Can I use the app without creating an account?

Yes, you can browse and search for restrooms without creating an account. However, creating a free account allows you to submit reviews, save favorite locations, and access personalized features.

How do you ensure review authenticity?

All reviews are submitted by verified users, and we have automated systems to detect and remove fake or spam reviews. Users can also flag suspicious content for our moderation team to review.

Is my location data private?

Absolutely. We take privacy seriously and never share your location data with third parties. Location information is only used to provide you with relevant restroom suggestions and is encrypted for your protection.

What if I find incorrect information?

You can report incorrect information directly through the app. Our team reviews all reports and updates the database accordingly. You can also suggest edits to existing listings to help keep information current.
