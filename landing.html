Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the location data?
Our app uses GPS technology combined with user-submitted data to provide highly accurate location information. We continuously verify and update locations based on community feedback to ensure reliability.
Can I use the app without creating an account?
Yes! You can browse restroom locations and read reviews without an account. However, creating a free account allows you to submit reviews, upload photos, and save favorite locations.
How can I add a new restroom location?
Simply tap the "Add Location" button in the app, enter the address or drop a pin on the map, and provide basic information about the restroom. Our team will review and approve new locations within 24 hours.
Is my location data private?
Absolutely. We only use your location data to show nearby restrooms and never share it with third parties. You can review our privacy policy for complete details on data handling.
What if I find incorrect information?
You can report incorrect information directly through the app by tapping "Report an Issue" on any location page. Our team will investigate and update the information promptly.
